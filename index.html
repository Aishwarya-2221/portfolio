About

Hello, my name is Aishwarya Payas and I am a 10th grade student in Mumbai. I enjoy painting, dancing, and reading books, and my favourite place to be is at home, although I hope to travel to New York in the future.

I enjoy developing web applications and have learnt to code in languages such as HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, and Bootstrap. Using these, I've created applications such as a tea station, a MI clone, a comfy store website, and a netflix clone. I'd like to continue developing similar applications to broaden my skillset.
